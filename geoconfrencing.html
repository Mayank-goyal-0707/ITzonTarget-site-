Geofencing the Neighborhood

Webinar

Check out our first ITZ Webinar about addressable geofencing and its ability to deliver banners, video, “native” and streaming TV ads to target audiences.

Social distancing is keeping people at home and all the more reliant on their phones, computers and TV screens — both to entertain themselves and to address their daily needs.

Instead of going out to shop, they’re doing their searching, browsing and ordering online for food, products and services of all kinds.

For businesses, that means an opportunity to get their message out and capture some of that online action.
There are some exceptional digital tools for doing that. A campaign delivered by “addressable geofencing” enables a business to target every home in a neighborhood and send a message to people on their mobile devices, computers and even TV screens.
