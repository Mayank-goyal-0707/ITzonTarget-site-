Case study

Chronogram Media

Website Design

A sales website for a community-oriented media company in Hudson Valley, New York.

Client Overview

Chronogram Media is made up of five publications that focus on local life in the Hudson Valley. While they already have websites for all of their publications, they asked us to design them a new sales website that they’ll use to attract clients for all five publications

The Project

It was important to us that the home page shows Chronogram’s community focus. We also knew that each publication would need a page with stats about distribution and images of recent covers, and the client asked us to create blog posts about some of their most recent community events.

The Results

The end result is a site that emphasizes their community focus and shows off the opportunities that Chronogram Media offers their clients.
