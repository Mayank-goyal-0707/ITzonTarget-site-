We made it easy
to get started

Step 1

Submit your project details:

This is where we get to know you and your project. Tell us all about it! This could involve either filling out a form or providing information to a team member to get started. We want to hear your ideas, your goals, and your vision. This step is all about gathering the information we need to create a plan or proposal that will help bring your project to life.

Step 2

Review our proposal:

After we've had a chance to get to know you and your project, we'll create a proposal or plan for you to review. This is where we'll outline the details of the project, like the timeline, budget, and scope. We want to make sure that we're on the same page before we move forward, so we encourage you to ask any questions or provide feedback.

Step 3

Approve the project launch:

Once we've ironed out all the details and made sure we're aligned with your vision, it's time to give the project the green light! By approving the project launch, you're letting us know that you're happy with the plan and ready to see it come to life.

Step 4

Monitor your campaign results:

Now that the project is underway, it's time to keep an eye on the progress and see how it's going. We'll be tracking everything to make sure we're meeting the objectives we outlined in the proposal. But we also want to hear from you - do you have any feedback or questions? We're here to make sure you're happy with the end result.
